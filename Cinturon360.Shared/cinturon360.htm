What Cinturon360 does.

Short version: it sits around your existing travel activity, enforces rules, and gives finance, HR, and risk teams one source of truth.

Connect

Ingest trips from GDS/NDC, TMCs, online booking tools, and even emailed itineraries.

  • GDS / NDC feeds
  • OTA / agency bookings
  • Supplier APIs
  • Webhook / file drops
Control

Apply policy per business unit, traveller type, route, airline preferences, or spend caps.

  • Pre-trip approvals
  • Soft and hard exceptions
  • Policy versioning
  • Audit logs
Report

Finance-ready exports and dashboards for exec and procurement.

  • Travel spend by cost centre
  • Top routes, top airlines
  • Exception analysis
  • CSV, XLSX, API out
